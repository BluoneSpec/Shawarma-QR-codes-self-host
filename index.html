Our Story
Welcome to Shawarma Palace, a cozy food haven born from a simple love for authentic Middle Eastern flavor and Kenyan street-style warmth.

What started as a small kitchen passion quickly turned into a community favorite — a place where aroma meets appetite. Every wrap, every bite, and every sizzling plate tells a story of dedication, spice, and freshness.

We believe great food doesn’t just fill the stomach — it fills the heart. That’s why we prepare each shawarma with care, using hand-picked ingredients, freshly grilled meat, and sauces made in-house daily.

From lunchtime cravings to late-night bites, Shawarma Palace is the go-to spot for anyone who loves real flavor, great service, and the feeling of home.

Because here, every shawarma is more than food — it’s an experience.
