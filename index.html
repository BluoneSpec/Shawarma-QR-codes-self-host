Our Story

From a small street stall to Shawarma Palace - our journey of flavor

Welcome to Shawarma Palace

Founded in 2015, Shawarma Palace began as a humble street food stall in Buruburu Phase 4, Nairobi. Our founder, passionate about authentic Middle Eastern flavors, wanted to bring the true taste of shawarma to Kenya.

We believe that great food doesn't just fill the stomach — it fills the heart. That's why we prepare each shawarma with care, using hand-picked ingredients, freshly grilled meat, and sauces made in-house daily.

From lunchtime cravings to late-night bites, Shawarma Palace is the go-to spot for anyone who loves real flavor, great service, and the feeling of home.
